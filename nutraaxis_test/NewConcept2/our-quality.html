Our Process

Our Quality Process

From ingredient to finished product, quality is built into every step.

1. Sourced with Care

We partner with trusted suppliers who share our commitment to quality, purity, and transparency.

2. Tested by Our CMO

Our manufacturing partners test every raw ingredient and finished product to ensure identity, potency, and purity.

3. Certificates of Analysis

Our CMO provides a COA for every batch — verifying it meets our exacting specifications.

4. Third-Party Testing

We independently test every batch through accredited third-party laboratories for an extra layer of assurance.

5. Quality You Can Trust

Only batches that pass every test earn our seal and make it to you.
